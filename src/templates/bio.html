Biography

Born in 1960, Arthur D. Books attended Yale University where he majored in Plant Psychology. He struggled to get employment in this area. In his free time, he penned his first novel, "A Farewell to Legs" in 1985. Spurred on by the success of this book, which sold over ten copies, he started writing full time and has since written several other forgettable novels.
